# Figma AI Prompt: InboxOS – AI Email Command Center

## Overview

Design a modern, premium SaaS web application called **InboxOS**.

The product is **not an email client**. It is an **AI-powered Email Command Center** that transforms a user's inbox into a daily productivity dashboard.

Think of it as a combination of:

* Linear
* Notion
* Superhuman
* Arc Browser
* Apple Dashboard
* Stripe Dashboard

The design should feel calm, intelligent, minimal, and highly polished.

Use soft shadows, rounded corners (12–16px), subtle gradients, generous whitespace, and a clean SaaS aesthetic.

# Overall Experience

The application should feel like the user has hired an executive assistant that quietly organizes their digital life.

The interface should immediately answer:

* What needs my attention?
* What should I do next?
* What am I waiting on?
* Who is waiting on me?
* What can AI do for me?

Every screen should emphasize clarity, speed, and actionable insights over displaying raw email lists.

The final design should be polished enough to resemble a production-ready SaaS application suitable for both individual professionals and enterprise customers.
